The Open Islamicate Texts Initiative Advanced OCR Catalyst Project team (more on this larger project [here](https://medium.com/@openiti/openiti-aocp-9802865a6586)) is proud to announce that we have received a HathiTrust Research Center Advanced Collaborative Support Award for our project entitled, *Detecting and Transcribing Arabographic Texts*. Team member [David Smith](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/people/david-smith/) led this proposal, with Matthew Miller, [Sarah Bowen Savant](https://www.aku.edu/ismc/faculty/Pages/profile.aspx?ProfileID=1&Name=Sarah+Bowen+Savant), and [Maxim Romanov](https://alraqmiyyat.github.io/) serving as co-Principal Investigators. 

For more information, see [the official announcement](https://www.hathitrust.org/htrc-awards-three-acs-projects) on the HathiTrust Digital Library site.
